General Controls

  • Use the Mouse Wheel to Zoom
  • Click a province to Zoom into it
  • Click on a check to Show Details about it
  • Click anywhere on the map to Hide the details tab
  • Right Click on a check to Mark / Unmark it
  • Click on a submap to Zoom into it
  • Zoom Out of a submap to Exit it
  • Right Click on a submap to Mark / Unmark all the checks inside it
  • You can Reset the Map in the settings

Dungeon Controls

  • Press [ W ] or [ ↑ ] to Move Up a floor
  • Press [ S ] or [ ↓ ] to Move Down a floor
  • Press [ E ] or [ → ] to Check / Uncheck the current floor
  • Hover on a floor button to Preview a floor
  • Click on a floor button to Select a floor
  • Right Click on a floor button to Check / Uncheck a floor
  • Zoom Out to Exit the dungeon map

Cave of Ordeals Controls

Keyboard Controls are the same as Dungeons

You can Hold a Direction Key to Move Through Floors Faster
  • Click / Right Click on the Up Arrow to Move Up a floor
  • Click / Right Click on the Down Arrow to Move Down a floor
  • Click / Right Click on the Floor Button to Check / Uncheck the current floor

Tracker Controls

  • Click on an item to Mark it
  • Right Click on an item to Unmark it
  • Middle Click on an item to Reset it
  • Click Anywhere on the Tracker to Close a Tracker Submenu
  • You can Reset the Tracker in the settings
