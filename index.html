General Controls

  • Use the Mouse Wheel to Zoom
  • Click a province to Zoom into it
  • Right Click a province to Set / Unset all the flags inside it
  • Click on a submap to Zoom into it
  • Zoom Out of a submap to Exit it
  • Right Click on a submap to Set / Unset all the flags inside it
  • Middle Click on a submap to Junk / Unjunk all the current shown and not set Flags inside it

Flag Controls

  • Click on a flag to Show Details about it
  • Click anywhere on the map to Hide the details tab
  • Right Click on a flag to Set / Unset it
  • Middle Click on a flag to Junk / Unjunk it (Only for Rando Checks)
  • You can Reset the flags in the settings

Hide/Show Menu Buttons

  • Hold Left Click to temporarily hide the targeted flags
  • Right Click to toggle the visibility of the targeted flags

Dungeon Controls

  • Press [ W ] or [ ↑ ] to Move Up a floor
  • Press [ S ] or [ ↓ ] to Move Down a floor
  • Press [ E ] or [ → ] to Set / Unset the current floor
  • Press [ Q ] or [ ← ] to Junk / Unjunk the current floor
  • Hover on a floor button to Preview a floor
  • Click on a floor button to Select a floor
  • Right Click on a floor button to Check / Uncheck a floor
  • Zoom Out to Exit the dungeon map

Cave of Ordeals Controls

  • Press [ W ] or [ ↑ ] to Move Up a floor
  • Press [ A ] or [ ← ] to Move Up to the next floor with a marker
  • Press [ S ] or [ ↓ ] to Move Down a floor
  • Press [ D ] or [ → ] to Move Down to the next floor with a marker
  • You can Hold a Direction Key to Move Through Floors Faster
  • Press [ E ], [ SPACE ] or [ NUMPAD0 ] to CSet / Unset the current floor
  • Press [ Q ] or [ NUMPAD. ] to Junk / Unjunk the current floor
  • Click on the Up Arrow to Move Up a floor
  • Right Click on the Up Arrow to Move Up to the next floor with a marker
  • Click on the Down Arrow to Move Down a floor
  • Right Click on the Down Arrow to Move Down to the next floor with a marker
  • Click / Right Click on the Floor Button to Set / Unset the current floor

Tracker Controls

  • Click on an item to Increase its state
  • Right Click on an item to Decrease its state
  • Middle Click on an item to Reset it
  • Middle Click on a boss to mark it as Required
  • Click Anywhere on the Tracker to Close a Tracker Submenu
  • You can Reset the Tracker in the settings
